Harith, as a writer and strategist

I will explain why your company sucks free of charge.
For a certain fee I will pretend to others that it doesn't.

Studios usually trust me to direct their naming, verbal identity, and copywriting projects.
I like to help teams think in terms of narratives, attitudes and behaviours.
I have been doing this since 2013.
